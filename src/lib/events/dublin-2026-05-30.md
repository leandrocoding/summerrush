Join us on Saturday, May 30, 2026 for a Hide + Seek game across the Irish capital, Dublin. The game uses the numerous bus routes around the city. Expect an unpredictable game, developing quirky strategies around the winding suburban routes. This game is being organised by Otaku and MrCalipso.

### Getting There

* **Meeting point:** This will be by [the Spire](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cWxALKE35uWhqf4S9) on O’Connell Street, the city’s busiest street for bus routes and quick access to the nearby Connolly train station.
* **Flights:** Dublin Airport (DUB) is well served across all of Europe, and is about an hour away from the city centre by public bus, or less by coach. Belfast Airport (BFS) is another option, a 3 hour commute by rail.
* **Trains:** There exists a direct Belfast-Dublin Connolly train which takes roughly 90 minutes. Dublin Heuston and Dublin Connolly can be accessed directly from nearly every major town and city via Rail in the Republic.
* **Ferry:** Dublin Port and Rosslare Europort has ferries to the UK and mainland Europe. The quickest is from Holyhead (2 hours), which is connected via rail
* **Transit tickets:** An adult or Visitor Leap Card is necessary for pain free travel across Dublin. More info can be found [here](https://about.leapcard.ie/about).

### Game Info & Map

* **Format:** Standard Hide + Seek rules with a Medium setup.
* **Playable area:** The County of Dublin is the playing area.
* **Hiding radius:**  500 m around the indicated station/stop of the hiding zone.
* **Map:** Checkout the embedded map below, or open the full version [here](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/edit?mid=1_460kjKVX67cRLuPySWfufgf4fqTjoU&usp=sharing).

### About Dublin
A walkable city, where most parts of the city is accessible within the hour from the city centre via bus or rail. There is an urban centre, plenty of parks and coastline for interesting and scenic hiding spots. It is recommended to bring water with you when you hide, as public fountains are far and few between
