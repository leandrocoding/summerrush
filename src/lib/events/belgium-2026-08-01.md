One year ago, the Benelux server hosted their first game with a game across the entire country Belgium. Now, 1 year later, we are doing it again. Join us on the 1st of August for a 2-day large game across the entire country of Belgium! We are using all 450+ railway stations of Belgium, making for a very large and diverse map, where a lot of different strategies are possible.

### Getting There

* **Meeting point:** We will at Brussels Midi station, the most important railway station in Belgium. From here, you can reach every corner of the country by train, making it the ideal starting point.
* **Flights:** Belgium has two main airports, Brussels Zaventem (BRU) being the main one and Brussels Charleroi (CRL) for low-cost airlines. Both have good, direct and frequent transport links to Brussels Midi station. Belgium also has good and direct railway links to other Western European airports like Paris (CDG), Amsterdam (AMS), airports in the Ruhr Area (DUS, CGN, DTM) and Frankfurt (FRA).
* **Trains:** As mentioned before, Brussels Midi is well connected to every corner of Belgium and a lot of Western Europe. You have frequent connections to all neighbouring countries, with some even connected by high speed rail!
* **Transit tickets:** If you do not live in the Benelux and travel by train to Belgium, we recommend getting an Interrail/EURail pass. Almost all trains do not require reservations, so just get on any train. Another option is getting a [Train+ subscription](https://www.belgiantrain.be/en/products/trainplus), giving a permanent discount on train travel in Belgium. You do not need to live in Belgium and from calculations we don't expect you to spend more than €60 to €70 for the entire weekend.

### About Belgium

Ah, Belgium. Everybody likes to make fun of them. Both the Dutch and the French laugh at Belgium. The polar opposite provinces make it a mind bending geopolitical maze perfect for high stakes psychological warfare. 

With more castles per square kilometer than any other country on Earth, the terrain offers incredible, historic high ground vantage points. Success requires mastering multiple regional transport apps simultaneously while keeping your brain sharp on some good quality Belgian chocolate blocks. Downloading a translation app, tracking the shifting languages, and maintaining a good mood are absolute requirements to conquer the Heart of Europe!
